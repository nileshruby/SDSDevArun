Signing Into CPS NeXT

  1. Enter your User Name and Password.


  2. Click Login.
    The following screen appears.

    An email is sent to your registered email address with a one-time password (OTP) that is valid for 10 minutes and three entry attempts. A timer appears on the screen that tells you how much time you have left to enter your OTP.

  3. Enter your OTP in the Enter OTP Here field.
    • If the time exceeds 10 minutes, the user must go back to the login page and enter their credentials to log on to the system. If their credentials are valid, then the secondary authentication page appears again.
    • If the time is not exceeded, but the user did not receive an OTP, the user can select Resend OTP to receive a new OTP. A new OTP is emailed to the user.
  4. Click Submit.

If authentication is successful, you are taken to your landing page of the CPS NeXT.

Tip: If authentication fails, an error message appears, and you are allowed to re-enter your information. You can make up to three attempts at entering your OTP. After three unsuccessful attempts, email authentication is locked for your account. Contact the JHA helpdesk to unlock your email authentication. You are sent an email notification when your administrator unlocks your email authentication.
