Unlocking a User's Authentication

The user is allowed three attempts to enter the correct OTP that they received through email. After three consecutive failed attempts, the user's email authentication is locked. As an administrator, unlock their email authentication using the following steps.

  1. Log on to CPS NeXT and navigate to Global Administration.
  2. Select the desired user.
  3. Click Unlock Email Authentication.
  4. Click Yes.
A notification email is sent to the user, and they can now attempt to log on again.
