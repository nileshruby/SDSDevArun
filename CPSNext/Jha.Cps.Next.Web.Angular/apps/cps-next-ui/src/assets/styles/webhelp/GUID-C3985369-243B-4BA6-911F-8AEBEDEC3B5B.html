Dashboard

The CPS NeXT Dashboard is your entry point to all available CPS NeXT products.

The following screen appears upon logging on to CPS NeXT.



Click the right or left arrows to view additional products.

Tip: Tooltips appear when you hover over specific sections of various screens. For example, if you hover over the arrow at the right of the screen, a tooltip appears informing you that you can click or slide to view more available products.

The following screen appears when you select Card Maintenance.



Tip: If you are not assigned a certain product, that product is not available for selection on your dashboard. In this example, the iPay P2P product is not assigned. If you hover over a product that is not available, a tooltip appears telling you the product is not assigned.
